About

One portal. Every system.

My Smart City brings traffic, surveillance, and emergency response into one operational view — so dispatchers, officers, and citizens can react faster.

Adaptive traffic signals

Phase timings auto-adjust based on live density and emergency-vehicle preemption.

Citywide CCTV

Encrypted streams from 128 fixed cameras and 24 mobile units, all in one wall.

Unified response

Ambulance, fire, and police dispatch share one map — average response down 32%.

Open data feed

Anonymous traffic and air-quality data published daily for researchers and the public.
